## Full description (max 4000)
Vintract Mobile is the on-the-floor companion to the Vintract Industry 4.0 platform — built so operators in real factories can run inventory and production work straight from their phone.

The full Vintract platform is a web-based ERP for manufacturers: live material movement tracking, BOM-driven production planning, vendor management, waste analytics and team operations. The mobile app focuses on the actions that genuinely belong in your hand while you walk the shop floor.

WHAT YOU CAN DO ON YOUR PHONE
• Scan any SKU barcode or QR code through the camera and see stock, vendor, threshold and movement history in one glance.
• Receive inward stock as it lands at the warehouse — scan, enter quantity, save.
• Move stock from store to the production floor — same scan-then-qty flow with safeguards if you go below available stock.
• View live production orders, mark them started, save partial progress, and close them when complete.
• Log waste fast — damage, QC reject, scrap, expiry — with a one-tap reason and an automatic stock decrement that keeps the ledger honest.
• Change your password and manage your account from Profile.

UNIVERSAL APP, MANY FACTORIES
Vintract Mobile is one app for many companies. At login you enter your workspace code (e.g. motley-hsr), your work email and password. The app resolves your factory's backend automatically and keeps every team's data isolated.

OPENS THE WEB APP WHEN IT MAKES SENSE
Heavy editing flows — categories tree, bill-of-materials, vendor profiles, reports with XLSX export, user admin — stay in the Vintract web app where the bigger screen actually helps. The side menu shows you which features run natively on mobile and which open in your phone's browser, so nothing feels missing.

DESIGNED FOR THE INDIAN FACTORY
Built for Indian SMB manufacturers running real production lines. Light theme, big tap targets, fast scan-driven workflows, and an aesthetic that doesn't feel like a database form.

REQUIREMENTS
• Android 7 (Nougat) or later
• Camera access for scanning
• Your factory administrator will give you your workspace code and login

PRIVACY
The app stores your session token in the device's secure storage (Android Keystore). No location data, no third-party analytics. Camera frames are processed on-device and never saved. Full policy at https://vintract.com/privacy.html.
